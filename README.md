✍️ Essay Writing Practice App
A lightweight, secure Python-based word processor designed for Cambridge B2 First (FCE) students and teachers. This app creates a controlled writing environment for homework and exams, ensuring students produce original work without the help of copy-pasting or external AI tools.

✨ Key Features
Anti-Cheat Measures: Disables Copy, Paste, and Right-Click to ensure the essay is typed manually.

Time Tracking: Records the exact duration from the first keystroke to submission.

Live Word Count: Helps students hit the 140–190 or 200-word targets required for B2 exams.

Full-Screen Mode: Minimizes distractions by locking the interface into a kiosk-style view.

Automated Submission: Sends the essay, word count, and time statistics directly to the teacher's email.
